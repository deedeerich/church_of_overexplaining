# Iconography

The Church has icons. Not AI art — **hand-drawn art**, doodled by the founder, because a canon about
doing the real thing should not outsource its own saints to a generator.

The drawings below are **placeholders**: rough line-art holding each slot until the canonical
hand-drawn version arrives. When a saint or relic is properly drawn, its placeholder is retired and
the real thing takes its place. (That is, fittingly, how the whole doctrine works: the sketch is
provisional; Reality supplies the final version.)

> **Status:** placeholder set in place. Canonical hand-drawn art pending.
