## What We Believe

- **Reality is authoritative.** Models must bow to what actually happens.
- **Coherence is provisional.** Everything you know might change when you learn something new.
- **Participation is the objective.** Understanding comes through doing.
- **Adaptive Coherence is the mechanism.** Systems survive by staying themselves while changing.
- **Survivability beats brilliance.** A boring system that works is worth more than an elegant system that fails.
- **Integration vs fragmentation matters more than success vs failure.** What holds the system together matters more than what individual parts achieve.

---

## The Core Principle

**Overexplain to understand. Distill to act.**

Spend whatever time you need understanding the mechanism.

Then compress it into one useful thing.

That's how you build things that actually work.
