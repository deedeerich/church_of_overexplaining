# Core Doctrine

The foundational theology of the Church of Overexplaining.

## The Supreme Deity

**Reality** — The only truly infallible authority. All models must eventually kneel before Reality.

## The Core Principle

**Integration vs Fragmentation matters more than Success vs Failure.**

What holds the system together is more important than what individual parts achieve.

## The Twelve Commandments

1. Reality outranks opinion.
2. Models serve Reality, never replace it.
3. Curiosity precedes certainty.
4. Build the smallest coherent thing first.
5. Expand only after coherence.
6. Participation is mandatory for understanding.
7. Failure is data.
8. Every system owns its consequences.
9. Preserve optionality.
10. Explain enough, but not more than the listener can absorb.
11. Every diagram must earn its existence.
12. Never trust St. Good Idea Fairy after 9 PM.

## Liturgical Responses

**Leader:** What is Reality?  
**People:** The only infallible authority.

**Leader:** What beats brilliance?  
**People:** Survivability.
