# Saints and Archangels

The canonized principles of the Church.

## The Archangels

- **Archangel Curiosity** — The First Explorer. Ask the first question.
- **Archangel Stewardship** — Guardian of what has been built.
- **Archangel Participation** — Patron of builders. "Get on the ice."

## Key Saints

- **St. Good Idea Fairy** 😈✨ — Patron of wonderful distractions (cannot be trusted unsupervised)
- **St. Adaptive Coherence** 🍄🌲 — Patron of making the pieces fit
- **St. Kintsugi** 🏺✨ — Patron of recovery and iteration
- **St. Survivability** ⚙️ — Patron of enduring architectures
- **St. Reality Check** 🪞 — Patron of testing assumptions
- **St. Progressive Disclosure** 📚 — Patron of not overwhelming anyone
- **St. Minimum Coherent Slice** ✂️ — Patron of shipping
