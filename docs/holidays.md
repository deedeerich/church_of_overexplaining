# The Liturgical Calendar

Holy days and sacred observances.

## Sacred Observances

- **Feast of St. Good Idea Fairy** — When you say "this shouldn't take long"
- **Day of Adaptive Coherence** — When the plan changes and becomes better
- **Reality Day** — Immediately after production deployment
- **Minimum Coherent Slice Week** — Everyone ships something, nobody gold-plates
- **Festival of Participation** — Nobody discusses, everyone builds
- **Great Refactoring** — Annual cleanup, celebrate negative LOC
- **Feast of the Ice** — First day back after a break
- **Recovery Sunday** — Test backups, restore, sleep
