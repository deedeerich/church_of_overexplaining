# Canonical Proverbs

Pithy truths from the Church.

## On Building

- Working beats perfect.
- Survivability beats brilliance.
- Build the smallest coherent thing.
- Every diagram must earn its existence.
- Expansion follows integration.

## On Learning

- Participation precedes understanding.
- Curiosity is cheaper than certainty.
- Failure is data.
- The signal was always there.

## On Systems

- The map is not the territory.
- Thou shalt not mistake a coherent diagram for reality.
- Integration vs fragmentation matters more than success vs failure.

## On Skating

- The edge tells the truth.
- The body keeps the receipts.
- Expansion through momentum.
- Continuity is participation.
