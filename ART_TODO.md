# Canonical Art — Dee Dee's to-draw list

These are **yours to draw by hand.** Raw art, not AI-generated — that was the whole point. The SVG
files currently in `docs/assets/img/placeholder/` are throwaway line-art holding the slots. When you
draw the real one, it retires the placeholder.

## How the swap works (per drawing)

1. Draw it on paper. Scan or photograph it (clean, high-contrast; white or transparent background is nicest).
2. Save it as `docs/assets/img/canonical/<name>.png` (or `.jpg`/`.svg`) using the **filename** listed below.
3. Tell me "swap in the canonical <name>" (or edit `docs/illustrations.md` yourself to point `src` at
   `assets/img/canonical/<name>.png` and drop the *placeholder* caption).
4. Commit + push from the repo: `cd church_of_overexplaining && git add -A && git commit && git push origin main`.

You can do them one at a time — each drawing that lands just upgrades its slot. No need to finish all six at once.

## The list (canonical set, from the founding canon)

- [ ] **The Cathedral** — `cathedral.png`
  - *Depicts:* the home of the Church; the "Enter the Cathedral" icon.
  - *Iconography notes:* a little church/monastery; arched door; a rose window; a cross on top. Warm,
    inviting, slightly wonky — a place you'd actually want to enter, not a corporate HQ.
  - *Appears:* Iconography page; candidate for the homepage header.

- [ ] **St. Good Idea Fairy** 😈✨ — `good-idea-fairy.png`
  - *Depicts:* the patron of "this should only take five minutes" and 11:47 PM inspiration.
  - *Iconography notes:* a mischievous winged sprite — a lightbulb with little wings and a devilish
    sparkle works; bonus points if she's clearly about to cause a delightful problem.
  - *Appears:* Saints page; Iconography page.

- [ ] **The Holy Whiteboard** — `whiteboard.png`
  - *Depicts:* the sacred surface where every important conversation gets a diagram.
  - *Iconography notes:* a whiteboard on legs with two boxes, an arrow between them, and one honest scribble.
  - *Appears:* Iconography page; candidate for "Day of the Diagram" on the Holidays page.

- [ ] **The Diagram of Infinite Boxes** — `infinite-boxes.png`
  - *Depicts:* the cautionary relic — the architecture sin of too many boxes, all connected to everything.
  - *Iconography notes:* five-plus boxes with every possible line drawn between them; beautiful, unmaintainable,
    slightly menacing. Pairs with the parable "The Architect Who Drew Too Many Boxes."
  - *Appears:* Iconography page; candidate for the Heresies page and the Parables page.

- [ ] **The Sacred Coffee Mug** — `coffee-mug.png`
  - *Depicts:* "Coffee is encouraged." The fuel of the Church.
  - *Iconography notes:* a mug with three curls of steam; optional tiny architecture diagram printed on the side.
  - *Appears:* Iconography page; candidate for the homepage footer.

- [ ] **The Blessed Sticky Note** — `sticky-note.png`
  - *Depicts:* "Distill to act" — the one cue that fits on a sticky note after all the overexplaining.
  - *Iconography notes:* a square note with a curled corner and one short line of writing. The physical form
    of the Minimum Coherent Slice.
  - *Appears:* Iconography page; candidate next to "Overexplain to understand. Distill to act." in the doctrine.
